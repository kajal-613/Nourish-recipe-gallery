1 Information We Collect

When you use Nourish, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and password (stored securely as a hash).
  • Recipe Data: Recipes, reviews, and ratings you submit to the platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the site, such as recipes viewed and searches performed.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security purposes.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Nourish platform and its features.
  • Personalise your experience — such as showing your saved favorites.
  • Send important account and security notifications.
  • Respond to your support requests and messages.
  • Monitor and analyse usage patterns to improve the service.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your privacy matters to us.

3 Information Sharing

Nourish does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly agree to share information.
  • For legal reasons: When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Service providers: Trusted third-party services (like cloud hosting) that help us operate the platform, bound by confidentiality agreements.

4 Cookies

Nourish uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to store your preferences, login token, and grocery list on your device. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly submit it to our servers.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or logging out of your account.

5 Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with expiration dates.
  • All data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas with access controls.
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information at any time.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@nourish.app.

7 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us:

  • Email: hello@nourish.app
  • Contact form: nourish.app/contact

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
